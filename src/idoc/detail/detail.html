George Triadafilopoulos' Background

BIO

Dr. George Triadafilopoulos is Clinical Professor of Medicine at the Division of Gastroenterology and Hepatology, Stanford University School of Medicine, Stanford, CA. He is certified in Internal Medicine and Gastroenterology by the American Board of Internal Medicine. Dr. Triadafilopoulos’s research activity ranges from gastroesophageal reflux disease and Barrett’s esophagus, to NSAID-induced gastropathy, colon polyps, C.difficile infection, motility disorders and irritable bowel syndrome
